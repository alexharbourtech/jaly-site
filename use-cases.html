Applications

Use Cases.

The most common applications Jaly deploys across commercial, industrial, and residential clients.

Energy Management

Energy Metering

Single-phase and three-phase metering on main incomers, sub-circuits, or individual loads. Real-time consumption data per device or zone.

Out-of-Hours Control

Automated switching of heating, equipment, and significant loads based on time schedules. Manual override always available on-site.

Load Management

Monitor and control high-consumption loads — transformers, immersion heaters, industrial equipment. Staggered start-up to prevent power spikes.

Heating & Climate

Cabin Heating

Scheduled heating for site offices and cabins. Hardwired boost buttons for on-demand warmth. Cabin at the right temperature when the crew arrives, off when they leave.

Hotel Room Heating

Per-room or per-zone heating control integrated with occupancy. Centralised visibility across all rooms from a single Fleet Manager dashboard.

Thermostatic Control

Smart thermostats and in-line temperature sensors ensure target temperatures are met without overshooting.

Monitoring & Visibility

Fleet Management

All devices across all sites managed from one web-based Fleet Manager dashboard. Control per device, group, site, or globally.

Water Metering

Non-Shelly pulse meters integrated into the Jaly system for full water consumption monitoring alongside power data.

BACS Compliance

A properly designed Jaly system delivers BACS-compliant functionality. Required by S.I. 393 of 2021 for non-residential buildings above 290kW since 31 December 2024.
