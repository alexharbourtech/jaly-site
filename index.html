Regulatory note: Irish law (S.I. 393 of 2021) requires non-residential buildings with heating, cooling, and ventilation output above 290 kW to have a Building Automation and Control System (BACS) installed. The deadline was 31 December 2024. A properly commissioned Jaly system delivers BACS-compliant functionality. See how this applies to construction sites →
