Stage 01

System Design

Every project begins with a conversation — formal or informal, on site or remote. Once the intent is established and the brief makes sense, we visit to scope the project properly. We will not design a system from drawings alone.

The deliverable is a formal document: the system architecture, required hardware with quantities, indicative setup guidance, and associated hardware costs. It is a working document, not a sales proposal.

Design Fee Rebate

System design is a paid engagement. Clients who proceed with Shelly hardware through Jaly receive a rebate against their hardware order — effectively recovering a portion of the design cost. Rebate terms confirmed at the outset.

What You Receive

  • System architecture specific to your site and objectives
  • Full hardware specification — device types and quantities
  • Network and integration planning — WiFi, LoRa, gateway requirements
  • Indicative wiring and setup guidance for your installation team
  • Hardware costs with volume pricing applied where relevant
  • Coordination with your M&E consultant or contractor where required

Stage 02

Hardware Supply

As Ireland's authorised B2B distributor for Shelly, Jaly supplies genuine, warranty-backed hardware directly to your project or installation team. Every device sourced through the manufacturer's authorised channel.

Local stock held across Ireland. Volume discounts calculated on committed order size — not just what has shipped — which benefits larger projects from the outset.

Stage 03

Installation

Physical installation is not a core Jaly service, but it is never left unmanaged. We work with a trusted network of electrical and M&E contractors who carry out installations to the standard our commissioning process requires.

Where a client has an existing contractor relationship, we are happy to work alongside them.

Discuss your installation requirements

How It Works

Jaly coordinates, partners deliver. We manage the relationship between design, hardware supply, and physical installation — so there is no gap between what was specified and what gets installed.

  • Wiring diagrams and installation documentation provided
  • Jaly available by phone throughout the installation phase
  • Trusted partner network available where required
  • Existing contractor relationships accommodated
Stage 04

Commissioning

Jaly attends site to commission every system. Devices enrolled into Shelly Cloud for single-site projects, or Fleet Manager for commercial and multi-site deployments.

Fleet Manager allows configurations to be pre-loaded and pushed to devices as they come online — WiFi credentials, groupings, automation rules — significantly reducing commissioning time on large rollouts.

Scope defines what commissioning includes. We will not quote until the scope is agreed.
