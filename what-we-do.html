Services

What We Do.

System design, hardware supply, installation, commissioning, and managed support. Everything required to go from a problem statement to a working building automation system.

01

System Design

We visit your site and produce a full system architecture — hardware specification, quantities, wiring guidance, and a cost plan.

Deliverable: Architecture doc, bill of materials, cost plan
Note: Paid engagement. Hardware rebate on proceeding.
02

Hardware Supply

Authorised Shelly B2B distributor for Ireland. Local stock. Volume pricing based on committed order size — not just shipped volume.

Pricing: Shelly RRP with volume discounts
Stock: Local Irish stock, fast turnaround
03

Installation

Carried out by our partner network of qualified M&E contractors. Jaly coordinates, provides wiring diagrams, and offers phone support.

Partners: Qualified M&E contractors across Ireland
Support: Wiring diagrams and on-call technical guidance
04

Commissioning

Jaly attends site to enrol and configure all devices. Fleet Manager allows pre-loaded configurations to be pushed at scale across large rollouts.

Includes: Automations, dashboards, MQTT, custom UI per scope
Fleet: Pre-built configs pushed across all sites simultaneously
05

Managed Support

Ongoing support, monitoring, and system management available by agreement. System is handed over fully working in all cases.

Available: By agreement after commissioning
Scope: Monitoring, adjustments, additions
