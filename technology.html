Technology

The Shelly platform,
explained for
commercial buyers.

Shelly is one of the most capable IoT platforms available — but it is widely associated with consumer and DIY use. Jaly exists to deploy it properly, at commercial and industrial scale, with the design and commissioning rigour those environments require.

Three Capabilities

Control. Automation. Monitoring.

01 / Control
Control

Direct switching, dimming, and actuation of electrical loads — from individual circuits to multi-zone estates. Local and remote operation with no perceptible delay.

  • Switch any electrical load on or off — individually or in groups
  • Dim lighting circuits from 0–100%
  • Control roller blinds, shutters, and motorised valves
  • Operate heating at TRV, spur, or zone level
  • Remote control from app, web, or third-party system
02 / Automation
Automation

Condition-based logic and schedules that run without cloud dependency. Once configured, automations execute locally — no internet required, no single point of failure.

  • Time-based schedules — days, hours, minutes, exceptions
  • Sensor-triggered rules — temperature, humidity, motion, occupancy
  • Inter-device triggers — one device action triggers another
  • JavaScript scripting for complex custom logic
  • Fleet-level automation pushed from central dashboard
03 / Monitoring
Monitoring

Real-time visibility into energy consumption, device status, and environmental conditions. Data viewable in-app, exported, pushed to third-party systems, or presented in a custom dashboard.

  • Energy metering per circuit — watts, kWh, cost
  • Single and three-phase metering available
  • Temperature and humidity monitoring
  • Device status and connectivity alerts
  • Data export, MQTT push, API access
Why Shelly

What makes it genuinely different.

There are many IoT platforms. Most are proprietary, expensive, or designed for a narrow application. Shelly's combination of open architecture, broad device range, and professional-grade management tools makes it unusual.

See it in use on our projects
01
Retrofit without rewiring

Shelly devices install behind existing switches and sockets. No new cabling, no visible changes. Smart capability added to existing electrical infrastructure at a fraction of proprietary BMS cost.

02
Open by default

Full REST API, MQTT, webhooks, WebSocket. Shelly connects to almost any third-party system — BMS software, energy monitoring platforms, property management systems. No lock-in, no integration fees.

03
Same platform, any scale

Fleet Manager controls and monitors from one device to thousands across multiple sites. The same dashboard used by a hotel for 100 rooms is used by a contractor for a national site network.

04
No subscription. No cloud dependency.

Shelly devices operate locally by default. No monthly fees, no vendor dependency, no system failure if the internet goes down.

Connectivity and Integration

It connects to what you already have.

Shelly devices communicate across multiple protocols and connect to the platforms your business already uses. Where a custom integration is needed, Jaly can build it.

WiFi

Primary connectivity. Operates on existing building WiFi — no new network hardware required in most cases.

LoRa

Long-range, low-power connectivity for devices outside WiFi range. Up to 5km. Suitable for outdoor, remote, or large industrial sites.

MQTT

Industry-standard IoT messaging. Push energy and status data to any MQTT broker — BMS software, custom dashboards, or third-party analytics.

REST API

Full documented API across the entire product range. Query any device, push commands, retrieve data. Custom integrations built by Jaly where required.
